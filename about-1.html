MY MAKE-UP JOURNEY

Growing up I had really ugly acne scars on my face,
I was in and out of Dermatologists because I couldn't
bare looking at my face in a mirror. That's when I fell
inlove with the beauty of the art of make-up, I felt like it had
restored my confidence back with make-up on my face I felt like
I didn't own any acne acne scars. Sooner or later I had to heal my
acne scars, so I'm still in the process of healing and removing
the dark spots left but my face is so much better than it was.
My business has been running for 5 years now, but when it
started I faced a lot of difficulties but as time passed I got
the hang of everything. My family has been supportive from the
word "GO" and I'm also planning on opening my first branch this year.
